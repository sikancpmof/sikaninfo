General Android Tips

  • Developer Options: Enable to adjust animation scales and DPI
  • Game Mode: Most Android phones have a game mode or similar feature
  • Background Apps: Close all unnecessary apps before playing
  • Screen Refresh Rate: Set to highest available option
  • Touch Sampling Rate: Increase if your device supports it
